2025 MLB Season Homerun Leaders

By Jack Zwirn

November 17, 2025

The 2025 MLB season is over, and now is the time to look back at who led what statistical categories. The first chart shows how homeruns were distributed across all 30 teams in the MLB, while the second focuses in on Cal Raleigh.

Raleigh is a catcher for the Seattle Mariners, and led the league in homeruns this past season. It shows how his homeruns were divied up by what count he was in during his at bat.
